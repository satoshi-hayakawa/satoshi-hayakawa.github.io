Satoshi Hayakawa

This is Satoshi Hayakawa's research homepage. Japanese

I joined Sony in March 2024. I was a DPhil student at Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford from October 2020 to January 2024 (thesis). My research interest in PhD is applied probability, including mathematical statistics, numerical analysis and machine learning; random convex hulls and kernel quadrature in particular. I currently work on probabilistic generative models, in particular the diffusion models.
